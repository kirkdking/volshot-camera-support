Privacy Policy

VolShot Camera does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app requires access to:

  • Camera – to record video.
  • Microphone – to capture audio in videos.
  • Photos – to save recorded videos to your photo library.
  • Photo Album – to create "UP" and "Down" albums in your photo library.

All recordings are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.
